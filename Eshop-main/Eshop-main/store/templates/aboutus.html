About the Store

Elegant Oysters Online Store is an organization established by a few like minded individuals living under the same roof. Their passion for developing handcrafted oyster candles led to the formation of a group that share true passion towards the business as well as the project. The organization was established and registered on 12th May, 2018. Primarily the company had two shareholders owning the business. Now the company has two more owners.
